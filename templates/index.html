About MMVALI Farm

At MMVALI Farm, we believe good health begins with pure food. Every product we offer — from fresh milk and curd to paneer, kova and ghee — is carefully prepared using pure cow’s milk from our own farm.

We follow hygienic processing, responsible farming practices, and eco-friendly methods to maintain the natural goodness of milk. As a family-run dairy, we are committed to quality, transparency, and long-term customer trust.

Pure from our farm. Honest in every drop.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does your milk come from? ▼

Our milk comes directly from cows raised on our own MMVALI Dairy Farm without third-party sourcing.

Are your dairy products fresh every day? ▼

Yes, all our products including milk, curd, paneer, and kova are prepared fresh daily.

Do you use preservatives or chemicals? ▼

No. We do not use preservatives, artificial colors, or harmful chemicals in any of our products.

Is your ghee made from pure cow’s milk? ▼

Yes, our ghee is traditionally prepared from pure cow’s milk to retain natural aroma and nutrition.

How do you ensure hygiene and safety? ▼

We follow strict hygiene practices during milking, processing, and packaging.

Do you provide home delivery? ▼

Yes, we provide local home delivery. Please contact us for availability.

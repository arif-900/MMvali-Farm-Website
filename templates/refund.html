Refund Policy

At MMVALI Farm, we care about customer satisfaction and product quality. This refund policy explains when and how refunds or replacements are provided for dairy products purchased from us.

1. Eligibility for Refund or Replacement

Refunds or replacements may be considered in cases where:

  • The product is damaged or spoiled at the time of delivery.
  • An incorrect product was delivered compared to your order.

2. Reporting an Issue

Any issue must be reported within 24 hours of delivery. Please contact us with details of the problem and, if possible, share photos of the product and packing.

3. Verification Process

All refund or replacement requests are subject to verification by MMVALI Farm. We reserve the right to approve or decline a request based on the facts and evidence provided.

4. Refund Method

Approved refunds may be processed through the original mode of payment or via an alternative method, such as credit for a future order, as mutually agreed with the customer.

5. Non-Refundable Situations

We may not be able to offer a refund or replacement if:

  • The product has been consumed partially or fully.
  • The product was stored improperly after delivery.
  • The issue is reported much later than the stated time window.

6. Policy Changes

MMVALI Farm reserves the right to modify this refund policy at any time without prior notice. Please review this page periodically for updates.
